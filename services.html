Oncology Massage - For men and women

1 hour 15 minutes · $75

Helpful for all stages of cancer treatment and recovery, oncology massage is a safe and effective technique that can relieve pain and stress for many people. In 2006, while supporting my husband through treatment for cancer, I witnessed the benefits of massage as a complementary therapy for cancer patients, often referring to massage as "A Calmer Therapy" because it had such a profound impact without interferring with medications. Now trained and registered as an oncology massage therapist, I am pleased to offer this speciality to men and women in all stages of cancer treatment and recovery.

Pregnancy Massage

1 hour 15 minutes · $75

For mothers with low risk pregnancy massage is offered from conception to delivery. For high risk pregnancy, release is required from your LMC prior to treatment and will be offered from 14 weeks gestation. Benefits include: * relieving fatigue * reducing headaches and muscular pain * helps reduce stretch marks * gently prepares body for labour and delivery * relaxation helps control blood pressure * relieves sciatic nerve pain * strenghtens the immune system * improves lymphatic and blood circulation * reduces insomnia, stress and anxiety.
